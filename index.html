About Me

Hi! My name is Nathan Kendrick and I live in Kentucky, USA. I have always loved computers since my first Tandy 1000 computer as a kid in the 1980s. My first jump into HTML was around 1998 when I began making personal websites about some of my favorite video games. I fell in love with it early on and dreamed of it one day being a career. I made many websites using Notepad with the HTML I learned through reading forums and viewing source code, including some for local businesses during my high school years. I ended up in the military, however, and did not get to explore the wonderful world of web development again until many years later.

A couple years ago I brushed up on my HTML, graphic design, learned a little bit of CSS, and learned how to use Wordpress to create websites and blogs when I began my own business doing personal training as a NASM Certified Personal Trainer. With the practice from this, and newly learned skills in social media marketing I was able to demonstrate my abilities and get a job locally for a small business managing their social media platforms and creating and managing multiple websites for their various extensions of their company.

The changing economy due to the Coronavirus pandemic inspired me reevaluate how I saw my career going forward. I truly love this work and I wanted to set my sights on higher goals. I wanted to expand my abilities to a much greater extent and I wanted to challenge myself to accomplish this through taking on study at CareerFoundry. I enrolled in the Full Stack Web Development course and have devoted myself to full time study and practice. I will deepen my knowledge of web development by learning to write Javascript, learning all about Backend Development, best development practices, and so much more. I will hit the job market again fully prepared for a more robust web development career. I am open to remote and in person career opportunities and I feel CareerFoundry will absolutely set me on the right path.

I have a vast background in media creation and design. This is both from experience with work and other courses and classes I have attended but also from my hobbies. I have enjoyed making music in various Digital Audio Workstations such as FL Studio and Cubase, video production using Adobe Premiere, and photography, art, and web graphics for my own websites using Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop, Affinity Photo, and Affinity Design. I also enjoy travel, blogging, and writing.
