About Me

I have always had a strong desire to create things. It has given me an insatiable drive to delve into many creative fields from web design, photography, videography, traditional and digital art, coding, music production, and writing. Wherever my heart has taken me on my creative journey I’ve strived to become better and am continuously learning to perfect and expand into new skills. Employment, school courses, and my own personal hobbies over many years has given me a wide variety of experience in design and media creation.

Web Design + Development

I have performed both freelance and salaried/hourly work in web design and development. I studied Frontend Web Development at CareerFoundry and am currently studying to be a Full-Stack Developer. I am highly skilled at writing HTML and CSS to design beautiful, responsive and mobile friendly websites that deliver a product that meets the unique needs of my clients. I am also skilled at delivering WordPress websites for clients that desire their websites to be non-tech friendly and easily updatable by the client such as the case with blogs. I see websites as not only useful tools for accomplishing desired tasks but also as works of visual art that should be enjoyable and easy to use for anyone.

Graphic Design + Photography

I’ve performed work as an in-house designer, contractor/freelancer, and worked on many of my own projects over the years. I am proficient in Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Lightroom, Affinity Design, and Affinity Photo. I have designed for both print media and digital media. I am passionate about photography and enjoy shooting, editing, and sharing my work and helping other photographers bring out the best in their work.

Audio + Video Production

I have professional experience shooting and editing small budget television programming/commercials and social media content for YouTube, and Facebook use. I am highly skilled in Adobe Premiere. I have over sixteen years of experience writing and producing music for entertainment using Image-Line FL Studio and other DAWs.

Writing + Copywriting + Social Media

I enjoy writing and blogging as both a hobby and a line of work. I have 15 months of recent copywriting experience as part of a sales team for both print and digital/social media marketing. I am experienced in creating graphical and written content for Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn, Instagram, and other digital platforms as well as for physical (USPS, EDDM) and email mass marketing platforms such as Robly. I run my own blog, BackwoodsZen.com, discussing various topics about minimalism and mindfulness, as well as its corresponding social media platforms.
